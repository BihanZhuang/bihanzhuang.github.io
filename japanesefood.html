Why I love Japanese food so much

There is a huge variety of dishes, including sushi, sashimi, tempura, hibachi, ramen, etc. Major ingredients being seafood and vegetables, Japanese food is very healthy and might be the reason behind the long Japanese life expectancy (third in the world). Japanese food can be made so delicate that it deserves to be called an art. Most importantly though, the food is SO delicious!
